Description

Ultra-Reliable & Accurate:

VIVOSUN Thermometer & Hygrometer gauge are able to quickly report measurements every 10 seconds and has a wide measurement range of 14℉ to 122℉（-10℃~50℃), with a temperature accuracy range of around ±0.1℉ (±0.1℃). The humidity sensor measures humidity from 10% to 99%, humidity accuracy range around ±5%. Multiple Mounting Options: VIVOSUN Thermometer & Hygrometer gauge is designed with magnet attachment, table stand, and wall hanging function.

Easy to Read & Operate:

VIVOSUN humidity gauge is designed with a clean display and simple to use touch-screen, with a convenient touch panel to switch to Fahrenheit(°F) or Celsius(°C). WARRANTY: 1-year warranty.

  • Humidity measuring range: 10%~99%RH
  • Temperature and humidity resolution: 0.1℃ (0.1℉); 1%RH
  • Temperature accuracy: ±1 ℃（±2℉)
  • Humidity accuracy: ±5%
  • Measurement period: 10 S
  • Power supply: 3 Volt CR2032 button battery
  • Net weight: 47.5g
